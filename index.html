About Me


I'm an Electrical Engineer with 7+ years of experience. After my BS, I started my career by teaching courses of electrical engineering related to: Digital and Analog Electronics, Industrial Automation, Robotics and Control Theory.

Along with a wide range of technical skills, I am also versed in design theory and have an eye for aesthetic designs.

My passion is knowing how things works, from simple electronics to complex mechatronics/robotics systems. My interests lay in entrepreneurship/intrapreneurship, and I'm an advocate of the maker movement.
